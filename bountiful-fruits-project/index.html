About Delmar, SC

Bountiful Fruits is situated in Del Mar. Del Mar is a small coastal city located in San Diego County, Southern California. It has a population of around 4,500 people and covers an area of 1.8 square miles. Del Mar is known for its beautiful beaches, including the popular Del Mar City Beach, which attracts many surfers, sunbathers, and swimmers. The city also has a long and rich history, dating back to the early 1900s when it was founded as a seaside resort. Today, Del Mar is a popular tourist destination, offering a variety of upscale shops, restaurants, and cultural attractions. In addition to its beaches, Del Mar is also known for its scenic bluffs, which provide stunning views of the Pacific Ocean.
